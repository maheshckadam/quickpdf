Privacy Policy.

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Quickpdf processes your files entirely in your browser. Your files never get uploaded to our servers. We don't track you across the web. We don't use cookies for advertising. That's it.

1. What we collect

Quickpdf is built to collect as little as humanly possible. Here's the full list:

  • Anonymous usage analytics — page views and which tools get used, aggregated and anonymized. No personal data, no fingerprinting.
  • Server logs — your IP address and browser type are stored briefly by our hosting provider (Vercel) for security and performance, then deleted.

That's it. No accounts, no email collection, no marketing trackers, no advertising IDs.

2. What we don't collect

  • Your files. Every PDF tool on Quickpdf runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device. We can't see them, store them, or recover them.
  • Your passwords. When you use Protect PDF or Unlock PDF, the password stays in your browser memory and is wiped when you close the tab.
  • Your signatures. Drawn signatures live only in your browser. We never see them.
  • Personal information. No names, emails, phone numbers, or accounts.
  • Cross-site tracking. We don't follow you around the web.

3. How to verify our claims

Don't take our word for it. Open your browser's DevTools (press F12), go to the Network tab, and use any tool. You'll see zero file uploads. Everything happens locally on your device.

4. Third-party services

Quickpdf loads a few JavaScript libraries from public CDNs (Cloudflare, jsDelivr, unpkg) to keep the site fast and small. These CDNs may log basic request data per their own privacy policies, but they never see your files.

The OCR tool downloads a language recognition model from a CDN on first use, then caches it in your browser for offline use.

5. Cookies

Quickpdf doesn't use tracking or advertising cookies. If we use any cookies in the future, they'll be strictly functional (e.g., remembering your dark mode preference). We will never use cookies that follow you across the web.

6. Children's privacy

Quickpdf is suitable for general audiences. Since we don't collect personal information, the service is safe for users of all ages. Children under 13 should still have parental supervision when using any internet service.

7. Your rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there's nothing for us to delete or export on your behalf. Your data lives on your device — you already have full control.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page if our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revisions. Major changes will be announced on our blog.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out via the contact link in our footer, or open an issue on our GitHub.
